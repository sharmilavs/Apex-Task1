Popular Mango Varieties

Alphonso (Hapus)

Known for its sweetness and rich flavor, Alphonso is one of the most popular mangoes in India, especially from Ratnagiri, Maharashtra.

Dasheri

Originating from Uttar Pradesh, this variety is juicy and aromatic, perfect for enjoying raw or in desserts.

Totapuri

Widely used in juices and pulps, Totapuri mangoes are less sweet and have a distinctive pointed tip.

Banganapalli

These golden-yellow mangoes from Andhra Pradesh are large, fiberless, and excellent for direct consumption.
