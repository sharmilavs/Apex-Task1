Delicious Mango Recipes

Mango Lassi

Blend ripe mango pulp, yogurt, milk, sugar, and a dash of cardamom. Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Mango Salsa

Combine chopped mangoes with onions, tomatoes, coriander, lime juice, and a pinch of salt for a refreshing dip.

Mango Ice Cream

Whip mango puree with cream and condensed milk. Freeze until firm for a creamy summer dessert.

Mango Pickle

Spicy and tangy pickle made using raw mangoes, mustard, fenugreek, and chili powder. A perfect side for Indian meals!
